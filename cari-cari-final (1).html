Personal Data Protection Policy

1. Introduction

Cari-Cari respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This PDPA (Personal Data Protection Act) policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Business Information (if seller)
  • Location Data (GPS, with permission)
  • Transaction History
  • Device Information
  • Usage Analytics

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for:

  • Account creation and authentication
  • Providing marketplace services
  • Payment processing
  • Communication and support
  • Improving our services
  • Legal compliance
  • Preventing fraud and abuse

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Secure password hashing
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Regular security audits
  • Compliance with PDPA regulations

5. Your Rights

Under PDPA, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Obtain information about data usage

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request deletion anytime by contacting us.

7. Third-Party Services

We may share data with trusted partners (Supabase, Flexi Payment, Toyyibpay) only for service delivery. These partners are contractually bound to protect your data.

8. Contact Us

For PDPA inquiries or to exercise your rights, contact:

  • Email: privacy@cari-cari.com.my
  • Phone: +60-82-XXXXXX
  • Address: Kota Kinabalu, Sabah, Malaysia
